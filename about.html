Our Core Story

Igniting Global Change.

Kase was founded on the belief that the world's biggest challenges require the world's most innovative minds. We accelerate mission-driven companies ready to pivot from potential to global impact.

Annual Startup Impact

Over the years, Kase has accelerated a growing number of mission-driven startups globally, providing mentorship, funding, and resources to help them scale their impact.

Our Mission

The drive that pushes us forward.

Mission: Unlocking Scale

Our mission is to be a catalyst for positive change by providing a nurturing ecosystem that supports social entrepreneurs working in alignment with our thrust areas. Through targeted incubation, mentorship, and acceleration programs, we aim to cultivate groundbreaking solutions that address pressing global challenges and enhance the well-being of individuals, communities, and the environment.

Vision: A Better Future, Accelerated

Our vision is to empower and transform communities through sustainable social entrepreneurship, where innovation and technology converge to address global challenges. We envision a world where Agriculture, Environment, Equity, Wellbeing, Education, and Accessibility Tech innovations thrive, fostering equitable growth and positive social impact.

A Decade of Milestones

2015: Inception

The Pilot Program

Kase is launched in a small hub in London, focusing on local fintech startups with social mission. First cohort size: 5 companies.

2018: Global Expansion

Asia Market Entry

We opened our first Asia-Pacific office and expanded our mentor network to include over 50 impact investors, tripling our program capacity.

2021: The First Unicorn

Valuation Breakthrough

EcoStart, a climate-tech venture from our '19 cohort, achieved a $1.2B valuation, validating Kase's unique mission-first acceleration model.

2024: Impact Fund Launch

Kase Seed Fund I

Secured $150M for our dedicated seed fund, allowing Kase to offer more competitive funding terms and stronger follow-on support for our cohorts.
