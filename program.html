The Kase Acceleration Journey

Every Kase program follows a proven, intensive 3-phase roadmap designed to take your venture from validation to global readiness.

Phase 1: Foundation

Months 1–3

Focus: Refine your value proposition, validate product-market fit, and establish key KPIs through user research.

  • Initial Investment Check
  • Legal & IP Review
  • Core Team Building

Phase 2: Acceleration

Months 4–6

Focus: Scale your technology, execute growth marketing, and optimize operations for sustained expansion.

  • Growth Hacking Labs
  • Advanced Impact Modeling
  • International Pilot Preparation

Phase 3: Launch & Close

Months 7–9

Focus: Prepare your investor pitch, secure follow-on funding, and present at the Kase Global Demo Day to top-tier VCs.

  • Global Demo Day
  • Investor 1:1 Meetings
  • Strategic Partnership Finalization
