Apply to the Kase Accelerator.

We seek purpose-driven founders ready to scale their technology to solve global challenges. Applications are now open for Cohort 8.

Deadline: October 31st

Our 3-Step Selection Process

Initial Pitch & Screening

Submit your high-level pitch deck, team background, and foundational impact thesis. Our AI and Analyst teams perform initial scoring.

Duration: 2 Weeks

Deep Dive & Due Diligence

Selected teams provide detailed financial models, deeper impact metrics, and a full product roadmap for our review board.

Duration: 4 Weeks

Final Interview & Offer

The top teams meet with Kase partners for an in-person or virtual interview. Final selection and offer letters are extended.

Duration: 1 Week

Key Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a **for-profit startup** utilizing scalable technology (AI, SaaS, BioTech, etc.).
  • Demonstrate an **MVP (Minimum Viable Product)** or existing market traction (Seed to Series A stage).
  • Clear alignment with **one or more of the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)**.
  • Founding team of **2 or more dedicated individuals** with complementary skills.
  • We generally do not accept pre-seed, non-profit, or purely service-based businesses.
